Walking around the earth

Usage

  1. Input a name of place where you want to walk around and type enter key. You will go there on the map.
  2. Click starting point and ending point on the map. Odd-numbered click makes starting point and even-numbered click makes ending point. You can try to click repeatedly.
  3. Click the route button. The route and corresponding street view would be checked, then street view tour would start on Google earth plugin.
